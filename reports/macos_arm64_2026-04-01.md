## Observations

- All configs achieve real-time (RTF < 1) except faster-whisper medium on short audio (1.506).
- openai-whisper is fastest on Apple Silicon; faster-whisper INT8 kernels are not optimized for ARM.
- faster-whisper uses the least memory across all configs.
- torch-opt energy estimates are unreliable (psutil sampling misses torch.compile'd workloads).
- Medium model improves WER by ~0.5-2pp over small at 3x speed cost.
